Grilled Cheese Recipe

Everybody loves a classic grilled cheese sandwich! Easy to make and add ingredients to. I will teach you how to make my favorite version today!

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of any type of bread
  • A slice of cheader cheese
  • 2-3 slices of ham
  • Spreadable Butter

Instructions:

  1. Butter one side of each slices of bread
  2. Heat up pan on the stove and add the butter side down when heated up
  3. Add cheese slice on top of one of the pieces of bread and let it cook
  4. Add in the slices of ham to the pan to crisp it up and then add it to the other piece of the bread
  5. Once the bread is crispy, add the two pieces together and take it out of the pan
  6. Enjoy!
