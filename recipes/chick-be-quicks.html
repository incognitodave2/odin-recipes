Chick-Be-Quicks Recipe

Chick-Be-Quicks are a fantastic and easy chicken recipe that anyone can make that takes less than 40 minutes from preping to eating!

Make sure to have some sort of vegetable with this!

Ingredients:

  • 2 Chicken Breasts
  • 2 Cups Frenches Fried Onions
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 Cup of Flour
  • 8 Pillsbury Cresent Rolls

Instructions:

  1. Pre-heat Oven to 400 degrees
  2. Cut chicken into chicken tender size strips
  3. Put a cup of flour in a bag with the chicken tenders and make sure they are evenly coated
  4. Beat the two eggs until liquidy texture and dip the chicken into the egg
  5. Take the two cups of french fried onions and crush them up
  6. Roll the chicken in the french fried onions
  7. Roll the chicken in the pillsbury cresent rolls and put it on a lined baking sheet
  8. Brush the top with left-over egg wash
  9. Cook them in the oven for 25 minutes
