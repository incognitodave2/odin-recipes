Chick-Be-Quicks Recipe

Chick-Be-Quicks are a fantastic and easy chicken recipe that anyone can make that takes less than 40 minutes from preping to eating!

Make sure to have some sort of vegetable with this!

Ingredients:

  • 2 Chicken Breasts
  • 2 Cups Frenches Fried Onions
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 Cup of Flour
