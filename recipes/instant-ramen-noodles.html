Instant Ramen Noodles Recipe

Instant Ramen Noodles are one of my favorite quick an easy meals on a day where I'm feeling lazy. It takes less than 10 minutes from start to finish!

Ingredients:

  • Instant Ramen Noodle Package (Seasoning Packet Inside)
  • 3 Cups Water
  • Ice Cubes

Instructions:

  1. Boil 3 cups of water in a pan on the stove adding in the seasoning packet RIGHT away
  2. Crush up the ramen to a size you see fit and add the noodles in once the water is boiling
  3. Wait for 3 minutes constantly stirling the noodles to make sure they don't clump up
  4. Once finished, take the noodles and dump them into a bowl
  5. Add 1-2 ice cubes (depending on the size) to the bowl to cool down
  6. Enjoy!
